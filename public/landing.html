Motivation

Components

  • Parent Portal
    • Register Child.
    • Communicate with day care staff.
  • Staff Portal
    • Staff on-duty check in/out.
    • List of children in day care.
    • View the profile card of each child attending day care.
    • Notify Parents of any behavioral or medical issues.
